# Product Manual - Sample Document

## Introduction
This is a sample product manual used for testing the Gemini File Search Tool.

## Getting Started
To get started with your device:
1. Unbox the device carefully
2. Connect the power adapter
3. Press the power button for 3 seconds
4. Follow the on-screen setup wizard

## Safety Guidelines
- Do not expose the device to water or extreme temperatures
- Use only the provided power adapter
- Keep away from children under 3 years old
- Do not attempt to open the device casing

## Factory Reset
To reset the device to factory settings:
1. Go to Settings > System > Reset
2. Select "Factory Reset"
3. Enter your PIN code
4. Confirm the reset action
5. Wait for the device to restart

After factory reset, all personal data will be erased and the device will return to its original state.

## Troubleshooting
### Device won't turn on
- Check if the power adapter is connected properly
- Try a different power outlet
- Hold the power button for 10 seconds

### Screen is frozen
- Hold power + volume down for 15 seconds
- If unresponsive, wait for battery to drain completely

## Sensor Calibration
To calibrate the sensors:
1. Navigate to Settings > Sensors > Calibration
2. Place the device on a flat surface
3. Press "Start Calibration"
4. Wait 30 seconds without moving the device
5. Calibration complete notification will appear
